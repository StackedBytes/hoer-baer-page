## II. Data Processed
The app uses TelemetryDeck for anonymized usage analytics. Only anonymized or not directly personal usage data is collected, in particular:

- anonymized user ID
- app events
- timestamps rounded to the nearest hour
- technical device information (for example, device type and OS version)

Names, email addresses, and IP addresses are not collected or stored.

## III. Purpose and Legal Basis
The processing is used to improve app stability, functionality, and usability.

Where personal data is processed, the legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in improving the service).

## IV. Recipients and Storage Location
TelemetryDeck acts as the analytics service provider receiving the data.

Processing and storage take place on servers located within the European Union.

## V. Data Subject Rights
Data subjects have the rights under Arts. 15 to 22 GDPR and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular the Austrian Data Protection Authority.
